Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an excellent world class education using well trained teachers to build a total child. Our school is a community that emphasizes the love of God and joy of life. We value all children, staffs, parents and Government and welcome their unique contributions to our school.

Our Vision

Our vision is to educate young people to become model citizens of the world. As our contribution to the society and as part of our commitment to train up the total child, we have established FIRST ROYAL INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL worthy academically, morally, spiritually and socially.
