Few Words About Us!

FIRST ROYAL INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL is a private co educational day school. The school was founded in the year 2005. The school provide well-defined educational goals and moral training that caters for the developement of the individual child's talent, unique and potential vis-a-vis intellect and character. All resources are put together, both human and material to give a quality all round education to individual child through emphasis on high academic performances, social responsibility, job orientation, political participation as well a spiritual and moral values. The college maintain a very high standard in academic and co-curricular activities, which go a long way to enhance character building. Our students proceeds to world class college and universities in Africa, UK, and other part of the world.
